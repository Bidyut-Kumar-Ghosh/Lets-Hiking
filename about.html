WHO WE ARE

Let's Hiking was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to share their love for hiking and nature with others. What started as a small local hiking club has grown into a premier hiking adventure company, offering guided tours to some of the most breathtaking trails around the world.

OUR MISSION

At Let's Hiking, our mission is to inspire adventure, foster environmental stewardship, and create unforgettable experiences in nature. We believe that hiking is not just a recreational activity, but a way to connect with the natural world and with ourselves.

We are committed to:

  • Providing safe, educational, and enjoyable hiking experiences for all skill levels
  • Promoting environmental conservation and sustainable outdoor practices
  • Supporting local communities and economies in the areas we explore
  • Fostering a diverse and inclusive hiking community

OUR GUIDES

Our team consists of experienced, certified guides who are not only experts in outdoor navigation and safety but also passionate storytellers and educators. Each guide brings unique knowledge about local ecology, history, and culture to enhance your hiking experience.

All Let's Hiking guides are:

  • Certified in Wilderness First Aid and CPR
  • Experienced in the specific regions they lead
  • Knowledgeable about local flora, fauna, and geology
  • Committed to providing exceptional customer service
